Koshari

Egypt's national dish made with rice, lentils, pasta, and topped with spicy tomato sauce and crispy onions.

Price: 15-25 EGP

Ful Medames

Traditional breakfast dish of slow-cooked fava beans served with olive oil, garlic, and lemon juice.

Price: 8-15 EGP

Molokhia

Green soup made from jute leaves, typically served with rice and chicken or meat.

Price: 30-50 EGP

Mahshi

Stuffed vegetables (peppers, zucchini, cabbage) filled with rice, herbs, and sometimes meat.

Price: 25-40 EGP

Roz Bel Laban

Traditional rice pudding dessert flavored with vanilla and topped with nuts and cinnamon.

Price: 12-20 EGP

Ta'meya (Egyptian Falafel)

Deep-fried balls made from ground fava beans and herbs, served in pita bread with salad.

Price: 5-12 EGP

Prices may vary depending on location and restaurant. These are typical street food and local restaurant prices in Cairo.
